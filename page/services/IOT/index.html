Internet of Things

We are providing services as physical mechanics embedded with sensors, actuators connected with the internet and data in the cloud.

Agricultural Automatic Irrigation System

This IoT-based project focuses on developing a smart agricultural system that can perform and even monitor a host of farming tasks. For instance, you can schedule the system to irrigate a piece of land automatically, or you can spray fertilizers/pesticides on the crops wirelessly through your smartphone. Not just that, this IoT-based project can also successfully monitor soil moisture.

Security System

This IoT-based security system is programmed to monitor the entire floor of the building for tracking any kind of unusual movement. When turned on, a single movement could trigger an alarm, thereby alerting the owners of the property about unwanted visitors. It works something like this – whenever you vacate a house or a building, the Piezo sensor is turned on for tracking any movement in and around the property.

Home and Workplace Automation System

IoT-based home automation projects to automate the functioning of household appliances and objects over the Internet. All the household objects that are connected over the IoT network can be controlled and operated through your smartphone.

Outdoor Light and Circuit System

Street lights are fitted with LDR sensors that can monitor the movement of humans or vehicles in the street. If the sensor can catch any movement in the street, it signals the microcontroller, which then turns on the street light. Similarly, if there’s movement in the street, the microcontroller switches the lights off. This way, a substantial amount of energy can be saved.
